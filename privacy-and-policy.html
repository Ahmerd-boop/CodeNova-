Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you interact with our services, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Name, email and others
  • Payment Details: Billing address, payment method, transaction history
  • Communications: Support tickets, email correspondence, chat logs

Minimal Data Collection: We adhere to the principle of data minimization, collecting only what's necessary to deliver and improve our services.

How We Use Information

Your data enables us to provide exceptional service while continuously improving our platform:

Service Operation

Account management, template delivery, payment processing, and customer support.

Analytics & Improvement

Understanding usage patterns to enhance functionality and user experience.

Security & Fraud Prevention

Detecting and preventing unauthorized access and malicious activities.

Personalized Communication

Service updates, security alerts, and (with consent) promotional offers.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

No Data Selling: We maintain a strict policy against selling user data. Third-party access is limited to what's necessary for service provision, with robust data protection agreements in place.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you of any such change and outline your options.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use these technologies to enhance functionality and user experience:

Essential Cookies

  • Session management and authentication
  • Shopping functionality
  • Security features and fraud prevention

Marketing Cookies (Opt-In)

  • Ad targeting and conversion tracking
  • Remarketing campaigns
  • Social media integration

You can manage preferences through our Cookie Consent Tool or browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may impact service functionality.

Data Security and Retention

Our Security Measures

We implement a multi-layered security approach:

  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions and principle of least privilege
  • Infrastructure: Enterprise-grade firewalls, DDoS protection, and intrusion detection
  • Procedures: Regular security audits, penetration testing, and staff training

Your Privacy Rights

Exercising Your Rights: Submit requests to our Data Protection Officer at privacy@codenovatemplates.com. We respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect:

  • New service features or legal requirements
  • Changes in data processing activities
  • Evolving security standards

Notification Process

For significant changes, we will:

  • Post prominent notices on our website
  • Send email alerts to registered users
  • Provide advance notice when possible

Continued Use: Your ongoing use of our services after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage periodic review of this page for the latest information.
